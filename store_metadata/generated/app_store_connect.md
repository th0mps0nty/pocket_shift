## Description
Pocket Shift is a calm, supportive micro-habit app built around a simple counseling exercise: begin the day with coins in one pocket, then move one each time you notice yourself slipping into a negative loop.

The goal is not shame. The goal is awareness. Each coin becomes a small pause, a moment of honesty, and a chance to shift the tone of the day before it spreads any further.

Why people use Pocket Shift:
- One-tap coin flip from left pocket to right pocket
- Daily reset with a fresh pocket each morning
- Undo for the most recent move
- Local-only history and settings
- Custom reminders, sounds, haptics, and coin styles
- Supportive onboarding and about story

Pocket Shift is especially meaningful for Adults, parents, couples, and anyone building awareness around negative thought patterns.

Design principles:
- Awareness over shame
- Gentle repetition over perfection
- Private, local-first reflection
- Fast enough to use in the moment

Inspired by a counseling exercise shared by Brett Froggatt of Second Chance Columbus.
