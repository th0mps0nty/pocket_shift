## Full Description
A gentle pocket ritual for noticing negative loops before they steer the day.

Pocket Shift turns a simple counseling exercise into a calm daily ritual for awareness, perspective, and emotional reset.

Pocket Shift helps you:
- One-tap coin flip from left pocket to right pocket
- Daily reset with a fresh pocket each morning
- Undo for the most recent move
- Local-only history and settings
- Custom reminders, sounds, haptics, and coin styles
- Supportive onboarding and about story

Made for Adults, parents, couples, and anyone building awareness around negative thought patterns.

Local-first privacy:
Pocket Shift stores sessions and settings locally on device. No account, analytics SDK, or cloud sync is included in this build.

Principles:
- Awareness over shame
- A small shift is still a shift
- Fresh pockets tomorrow
