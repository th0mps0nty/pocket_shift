# Pocket Shift App Review Resubmission

## App Review Note

Pocket Shift has been substantially expanded since the previous submission and now provides a meaningful, standalone daily utility experience rather than a single-action interaction.

What is included in this version:

- structured daily sessions with persistent local history
- per-event trigger tagging when a user shifts a coin
- optional notes attached to individual shift events
- daily reflection prompts and saved reflection summaries
- a full Insights tab with weekly summaries, streaks, top triggers, and trend visualization
- detailed session drill-down from History
- reminders, settings, support, privacy access, and export/reset tools

Pocket Shift is designed as a local-first awareness and reflection app. No account is required and no server is required to use the core experience.

For review:

- Open `Today` to move a coin, tag the trigger, and add a note.
- Open `History` and tap a past day to view the session detail timeline and saved reflection.
- Open `Insights` to view the weekly summary, streak, trigger breakdown, and chart.
- Support and privacy links are available in `Settings`.

Pocket Shift is not intended as a novelty coin flipper. It is a repeat-use tool for capturing negative-pattern moments in context, reflecting on them, and reviewing patterns over time.

## Positioning Reminder

In App Store screenshots and metadata, avoid framing Pocket Shift as therapy, diagnosis, or punishment.

Prefer:

- awareness
- noticing patterns
- gentle reflection
- supportive reset

Avoid:

- medical claims
- shame-heavy language
- “fix yourself” framing
